Task: Recommend the top 3 crops for a farmer based on the provided location and conditions.

Inputs:
- Location: {location}
- Season: {season}
- Profit Priority: {priority}
- Soil Type: {soil_type} (optional)
- Water Availability: {water} (optional)

Requirements:
1. Recommend top 3 crops.
2. For each crop, explain WHY it was chosen for this agro-climatic zone (conceptually).
3. List 2-3 risk factors (e.g., pests, water stress, price volatility).
4. Provide a confidence level (percentage).
5. State your assumptions about the weather and soil if not provided.

Output Format:
- Use clear headings.
- Use bullet points for readability.
- Keep the language simple.
