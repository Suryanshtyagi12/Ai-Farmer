Inputs:
- Location: {location}
- Season: {season}
- Profit Priority: {priority}
- Soil Type: {soil_type} (optional)
- Water Availability: {water} (optional)

Requirements:
1. Recommend ONLY the top 3 crops.
2. For EACH crop, provide:
   a) Why suitable: 1-2 lines on suitability for this zone.
   b) Best Known For: one of Yield / Profit / Water Efficiency.
   c) Fertilizer Recommendation: Preferred type and simple usage stage (early/mid/late).
3. Be CONCISE. No long explanations.
4. If Soil/Water info is missing, state assumptions clearly.

Output Format:
### 1. [Crop Name]
- **Why Suitable**: [Explanation]
- **Best For**: [Yield/Profit/Efficiency]
- **Fertilizer**: [Type] at [Stage]
